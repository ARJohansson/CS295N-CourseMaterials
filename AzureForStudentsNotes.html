Azure student account experience

Went to Azure for Students

Clicked on "Activate Now"

Chose school e-mail as the validation method using birdb@lanecc.edu

Received an e-mail asking me to click on a link

Clicked on the link in the e-mail and got a page titled: "Azure for Students signup
Get $100 in Azure credits plus free access to 25+ popular products"

These are the features you get and their time limits: https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/free-account-students-faq/

  • Once the 12 months ends, or the $100 credit is used up, the 12 month free services, like 250 GB of SQL Database standard S0 instance with 10 DTUs ends!
  • 10 web, mobile, or API apps with Azure App Service with 1 GB storage are always free (for life?)

Clicked  the license agreement checkbox and then clicked on the "Subscribe" button

Was taken to the Azure portal and the Quickstart Center.

Clicked on Create an Azure service

  • Create a web app
  • Build and host a web app with Azure Web Apps
  • Entered the name of my app and created the app (at the top is a pink banner that says: "Your Free Credit account is about to expire. Click here to upgrade your trial account"
  • SQL Databases, selected Azure for Students account.
    • It only let me choose the Basic plan at $4.99 a month, no free plan was available.
  • Published to Azure without putting anything in the Migration field of the publish/configure dialog!
  • Can view the schema and data via the portal now
  • Check the remaining credit here: https://www.microsoftazuresponsorships.com


Hints for requesting support:

  • If you choose e-mail support, you don't have to be available at the time the support person calls back
  • If you choose phone support, be sure to also select your region, United States
